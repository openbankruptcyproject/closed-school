# closed-school.openbankruptcyproject.org

**Closed School Discharge**

Educational reference on the Closed School Discharge program for federal student loans, available to borrowers whose schools closed during or shortly after enrollment. Administered by the U.S. Department of Education.

Live site: <https://closed-school.openbankruptcyproject.org/>

Maintained by the [Open Bankruptcy Project](https://openbankruptcyproject.org/), a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 41-5159631).
